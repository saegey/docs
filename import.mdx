## Import activity

<Steps>
  <Step title='Select post in Monopad'>
    Navigate to one of your Monopad post pages and click the
    <Icon icon='ellipsis' iconType='solid' /> More menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title='Update post settings'>
    From the <Icon icon='ellipsis' iconType='solid' /> More menu, choose 'Settings'.
  </Step>
  <Step title='Upload activity'>
    From the Settings modal, click the 'Upload' button under Activity. Navigate to find the FIT file and upload it to Monopad.
  </Step>
  <Step title='Save changes'>
    Once Monopad is done uploading your file, click 'Save'.
  </Step>
</Steps>
