## Export from Strava

<Steps>
  <Step title='Select activity in Strava'>
    Navigate to one of your Activity pages and click the More{' '}
    <Icon icon='ellipsis' iconType='solid' /> menu on the activity you want to
    export.
  </Step>
  <Step title='Export file'>
    From the More <Icon icon='ellipsis' iconType='solid' /> menu, choose 'Export
    Original' and download the file.
    <Frame>
      <img
        height='200'
        className='block dark:hidden'
        src='/images/export-strava.png'
        style={{ borderRadius: '10px' }}
      />
    </Frame>
    The activity file will be downloaded to your computer in whatever format it
    originally uploaded. In many cases, the original will be in FIT (Flexible
    and Interoperable Data Transfer) format.
  </Step>
  <Step title='Upload'>Upload the FIT file to Monopad.</Step>
</Steps>

## Export from Garmin

<Steps>
  <Step title='Select activity in Strava'>
    Click the <Icon icon='gear' iconType='solid' /> icon on the activity you
    want to export.
  </Step>
  <Step title='Export file'>
    Choose 'Export File' and download the FIT file.
    <Frame>
      <img
        height='200'
        className='block dark:hidden'
        src='/images/export-garmin.png'
        style={{ borderRadius: '10px' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title='Upload'>
    Upload the FIT file to Monopad.
  </Step>
</Steps>
