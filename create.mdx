## Create a Post
<Steps>
  <Step title='Create a post'>
    Navigate to your Posts page and click the <Icon icon='plus' iconType='solid' /> button to create a new post. 
  </Step>
  <Step title='Update the post settings'>
    When your new Monopad post opens, you can update the post's settings by accessing the Settings modal using the <Icon icon='ellipsis' iconType='solid' /> More menu > <Icon icon='gear' iconType='solid' /> Settings option.
  </Step>
  <Step title='Start writting'>
   Start capturing your activity details.
  </Step>
</Steps>
